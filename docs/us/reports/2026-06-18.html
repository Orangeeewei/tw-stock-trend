Glossary (plain words)

Sector rotation
Money rotates among the 11 GICS sectors. This report tracks where money is flowing in, then hunts the stocks in that sector that haven't moved yet.
Off 60-day high
Distance below the 3-month high. Around -15% is a low base with room to catch up; much deeper, suspect the fundamentals.
Volume surge
5-day average volume vs the 20-day average; above 1.2× means buyers are showing up.
Momentum turn
A positive 5-day return that also beats the sector — the classic sign a laggard is waking up.
60-day moving average
Average cost of the past three months' buyers. S&P 500 above it = bull regime; below = bear. This is the report's master switch.
vs the Taiwan edition
The US lacks Taiwan's daily institutional flow and monthly revenue disclosures, so scoring here uses price/volume structure only. Prices are dividend-adjusted automatically.
